## How to Use These Prompts

Each prompt is copy-paste ready. Append your preferred style suffix at the end:
- **Midjourney:** add `--ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --style raw` (or `--ar 9:16` for vertical)
- **DALL-E:** prompts work as-is
- **Stable Diffusion:** add `photorealistic, 8k, sharp focus, studio lighting`

**Never add text to the image prompt.** All copy is listed separately as text overlay — add it in Canva, CapCut, or your editing tool after generation.

**Safe zones for platforms:**
- Meta 1:1: Keep key subject within center 80% of frame
- TikTok/Reels 9:16: Top 15% and bottom 20% should be minimal — TikTok UI overlaps these areas

## Generation Tips

**For best results:**
- Generate 4 variations per prompt and select the strongest
- Regenerate any image where the product label text appears garbled — request a clean label-facing-camera version
- If a human subject looks artificial or over-styled, add: `real person, not stock photo, authentic, imperfect`
- For Midjourney specifically, `--style raw` reduces the AI-polish look and produces more editorial/authentic results
- Concepts 1–3 should have noticeably cooler/cleaner color temperature than Concepts 4–5 — if they drift warm, adjust in Lightroom/Photoshop

**After generating:**
- Add copy overlays in Canva, Adobe Express, or CapCut
- Respect safe zones before placing text (see top of document)
- Test at least 2 copy variants per image in Meta (primary vs. secondary from campaign-brief.md)
